Our energy.

energy waste is a huge problem, and we can fix it by doing very simple things.

even if we only do one step, we are still changing the world.

1: we can turn off lights more frequently to save energy.

2: we can unplug items after use to save energy because some items still take energy for stuff like charging or "sleep mode".

3: we can use stuff like solar panels or windmills to create energy instead of using fuel and workers to make energy.

and finally, 4:we can use less electric technology or gadgets.

there are also other ways we can save the planet up above!
